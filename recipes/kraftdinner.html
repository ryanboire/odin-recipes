Kraft Dinner

Description

This recipe is for kraft dinner, in case instructions on box are unreadable!

Ingredients

  • Cheese Powder
  • Pasta from box
  • Tbsp of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add pasta noodles from KD box
  3. Boil for 8 minutes or until pasta is tender
  4. Strain water
  5. Add pasta back to pot
  6. Add butter, milk and cheese powder to pasta
  7. Stir until fully mixed
  8. Enjoy :)
