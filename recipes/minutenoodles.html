Minute Noodles!

Description

The following instructions are for minute noodles, with added soft boiled egg!

Ingredients

  • package of minute noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add noodles to water
  3. After about a minute, crack in the egg
  4. Let boil for about 2 minutes
  5. Strain half the water, leave half for broth
  6. Add seasoning packet
  7. Stir gently ensuring the egg does not break
  8. Enjoy :)
