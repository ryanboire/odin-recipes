Carnitas

Description

Carnitas is a simple yet delicious dish to make. The following webpage will include the ingredients along with simple instructions to follow to make this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs pork shoulder
  • Tbsp of cumin
  • Tbsp of salt
  • Tbsp of pepper
  • Tsp of cayenne pepper
  • Tbsp of garlic powder
  • Tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 chopped jalapeno pepper
  • 1 diced medium onion
  • 1 cup of beef broth

Steps

  1. Mix the spices in a bowl
  2. Chop jalapeno pepper into small bits (feel free to remove seeds to avoid being too spicy)
  3. Rub spices all over pork shoulder
  4. Place pork shoulder into slow cooker
  5. Pour beef broth over the pork shoulder
  6. Set slow cooker to low setting and cook for 8 hours
  7. After 8 hours, use forks to pull apart the pork
  8. Enjoy :)
